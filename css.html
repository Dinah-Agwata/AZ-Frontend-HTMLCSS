Importance of exercise

Regular physical activity has benefits regardless of age,sex or physical ability. Being able to move your body should be considered a blessing and not a chore that you have to do. Some of the benefits include controlling body weight, preventing diseases,it can improve your mood as a result of the endorphins released during a workout,it boosts energy, promotes better sleep and it can be fun and promote your social life.

Different forms of exercise

  • Yoga
  • Calisthenics
